Our Story

Discover the magic behind Luni Story Shop and our mission to inspire young minds through enchanting tales.

How It All Began

Luni Story Shop was born from a simple bedtime ritual. Our founder, Luna, noticed how her daughter's eyes would light up during storytime, especially when the tales were personalized and filled with wonder.

What started as creating custom stories for her own child soon expanded to sharing these magical tales with friends and family. The joy and imagination sparked by these stories inspired Luna to create Luni Story Shop - a place where every child could find stories that speak directly to their hearts.

Today, we're a team of storytellers, illustrators, and childhood enthusiasts dedicated to crafting stories that not only entertain but also nurture young minds and inspire creativity.
